The Ministry of Human Resource and Development (MHRD) has come up with a new plan of centralized Quality Circles for all IITs which aims to take the IITs (Indian Institute of Technology) to the next level in terms of research and higher education. The proposed Quality Circles at IITs will come up with new and innovative study curriculum, new experimentation, textbooks, workshops etc. in specific clusters of courses. MHRD also provides the faculty to take long leave for research work. The objective and goal of quality circles at IITs will be to take inputs and process them to design new course files, lecture material, compendium of problems and desired practical projects to be associated with the courses-said an official with MHRD. Apart from these, these cells would also work on developing experimentation kits for students to assemble and conduct basic experiments. This would help students understand the practical application and significance of what they study in the classroom.
