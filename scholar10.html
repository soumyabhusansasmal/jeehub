IET India Scholarships

Scholarship: IET India Scholarships

Institute: Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET), India.

Prize Regionwise:

Regional South 1: students from Karnataka & Andhra Pradesh

Regional South 2: students from Tamil Nadu & Kerala

Regional North

Regional East + North East

Regional West

Scholarship Category:

General Category

Women Category

Note: Women engineering students can apply for both the categories, but if selected in both the categories, she will receive award in only one category as decided by the IET Jury Panel.

Number of Awards:

1. General Category :

Two winners per Region (totally five regions)

Three winners at the National level

2. Women Engineer :

Two winners per Region (totally five regions)

Three winners at the National level

Distribution of Scholarship money :

1. Regional Level:

Winner - Rs.30, 000/- + Certificate

Runner - Rs.20, 000/- + Certificate

2. National Level:

First - Rs.75, 000/- + Certificate

Second - Rs. 60,000/- + Certificate

Third - Rs. 40,000/- + Certificate

Eligibility for Scholarship: Open only to students of B.Tech programmes doing their second, third and final year of engineering.

Application eligibility :

Students must be in their 2nd year of Engineering Applicant must have cleared all regular credit courses in a single attempt for all the semesters before submitting the application. UG marks should be at least 60% in aggregate for the semesters cleared so far or corresponding to CGPA of at least 6.5 in a 10 point scale. Higher Secondary (or equivalent) marks should be above 60% or of equivalent grade.

No age limit for the applicant. Scholarship is open for all IET members and non members and for all branches of engineering as recognised by the AICTE/UGC.

Applicants must fulfill the following:

Academics – 60%

Extra & Co-Curricular – 20%

Outreach – 10% (Activities like coordinating or organising industrial visits, organising lectures/seminars etc.)

Professional – 10% (Membership in professional bodies, coordinating activities of the professional bodies, office bearer of any professional body/student chapter, contributing to professional bodies through papers presentations etc)

Selection Procedure:

Stage 1: Log in to apply online for the IET India Scholarship Award Upload attested copies (by HOD/principal) of required documents are to be scanned and uploaded before March 15th 2013.

Stage 2: Students shortlisted must give an online test to assess the applicant’s knowledge in engineering subjects (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Computer Science, Electrical & Electronics.). The total number of questions will be approximately 40 with multiple choice options . Based on the cut –off (decided by the regional jury), the applicants will be shortlisted for the next level.

Stage 3: Regional Level Assessment: Students are required to give a verbal presentation to the jury on any selected topic of their choice or subject related to engineering. The jury will select the winners & runners-up for the regional level.

Stage 4: National Level Assessment: The final competition will be organised as an event at Delhi. Shortlisted candidates will make a presentation on their vision and solution, as they see it, for an engineering problem that is relevant to society. Students will present the solution in a Poster Presentation format in 15 minutes to be followed by Q&A Session.

Application deadines: March 15, 2015
