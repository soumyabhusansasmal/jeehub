JEE Advanced is the second part of Joint Entrance Exam - Eligible candidate from JEE Main will qualify for the JEE Advanced . Admissions to all IITs will be based only on category-wise All India Rank (AIR) in JEE-Advanced, subject to condition that such candidates are in the top 20 percentile categories.
