Navtej Kohli Scholarship

MASTER’s scholarship in engineering for students to study anywhere in India

Scholarship: Navtej Kohli Scholarship

Objective: To assist those wishing to pursue their higher education in engineering.

Financial assistance: Rs. 1 Lakh to one outstanding student

Eligibility:

Scholarship is open to students in the region of Delhi. Candidates must be of such an age that they will have passed their 22nd and not have passed their 28th birthday on the 1st October 2015. Must have a first class degree in B.Tech, BE or BCA an accredited college. Those who are in their final year of a degree course may also apply, however, selection will be conditional based on final year results.

Selection process: Applicants are assessed for their intellectual distinction, character, commitment to service, socio-financial status and technical acumen. First stage, all the candidates from Delhi region would send in their required details through our online application form. Second stage will involve screening process and personal interviews to shortlist candidates for the final interview.
