JEE Main (previously AIEEE) was introduced for the first time in 2013. JEE Main serves as the entry gate for admission in National Institute of Technology (NITs), IIITs ( Indian Institute of Information Technology), and various other central and private colleges. 40 % component of 12th Score is also taken into consideration.

JEE Main 2014 Important Dates list out the crucial dates pertaining to the Joint entrance Examination 2014. Candidates can get all the details about the Application dates, Exam Schedules, Admit Card availability, Results etc from the Important Dates of JEE Main 2014. Candidates can get all the Important Dates of JEE Main 2014 below. Please note that the articles are being updated as and when the CBSE issues the information.
