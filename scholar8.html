Narotam Sekhsaria Engineering Scholarship Programme

Scholarships awarded by: The Narotam Sekhsaria Foundation

Scholarship details:

1. Excellence Award & Certificates of Merit:

One Excellence Award of Rs. 1,00,000

Two Certificates of Merit of Rs. 50,000 each

Eligibility: Outstanding students from the final year engineering students of those engineering colleges included in the scholarship programme.

Documents to be attached for Excellence Award & Certificate of Merit: Attested copies of:

All mark sheets and certificates in support of your educational qualifications

MHT-CET/AIEEE/IIT-JEE Score cards

Award letters of scholarships/ awards/ prizes received

Certificate of participation at seminars / workshops /conferences

Bonafide certificate from college

Copy of college identity card

2. Merit-Cum-Means scholarships:

No of Scholarships: 100

Scholarship amount: upto a maximum of Rs. 30,000 each to be awarded to the undergraduate students of the engineering colleges included in the scholarship programme across all the four years

Eligibility:

Candidates in the first year of the BE/BTech programme must have obtained 85% and above aggregate marks in Class XII(HSC or equivalent). Candidates in the 2nd, 3rd and 4th year of the BE/ BTech must have obtained 70% and above aggregate marks in the previous year. Gross family income of candidates must be less than Rs. 50,000/- per month. Overall meritorious performance of candidates. Documents to be attached for Merit- Cum - Means Scholarships: Attested copies of:

All mark sheets and certificates in support of your educational qualifications

MHT-CET/AIEEE/IIT-JEE Score cards

Award letters of scholarships/ awards/ prizes received

Fee receipt of the current year

Bonafide certificate from college

Copy of college identity card

Proof of gross family income (Income certificate/ latest IT returns/ Form 16/ latest salary slip)
