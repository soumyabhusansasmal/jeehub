L&T Build India Scholarship

Sponsored by: L & T Constuction

Programme: M.Tech in Construction Technology

Institutes: Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi and Madras

Duration: 24 months

No. of scholarships: 40

Eligibility:

First-class graduates with minimum aggregate average of 65% of marks in Civil /Mechanical /Electrical Engineering who will pass out in 2013 from a recognized Indian University / Institute.

Age: Not more than 23 years as on 01-07-2013.

Minimum Height: 160 cm.

Minimum Weight: 50kg.

Vision: If wearing glasses, power should be less than ± 5.0 dioptres

Course Details: The Course is designed by the IIT's in consultation with ECC.

Selection: A series of Written tests, Group Discussion and Personal Interview to be done jointly by IITs & L&T.

Completion of Programme: On successful completion of the M.Tech. course, the selected candidates are absorbed in regular employment in accordance with the terms and conditions of the Scheme.

How to apply:

apply online

Note: Candidates are required to have a valid active personal e-mail ID and should not change the same during the course of the recruitment process to ensure smooth communication. Communication will be sent to such email ID.

Important: After the submission of the application form, a registration number will be generated by the system. Kindly note the same for future use.
