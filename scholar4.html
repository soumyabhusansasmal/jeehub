Sahu Jain Trust - Educational Scholarships

(A) Inland Scholarship for pursuing

(a) Technical Trade Courses including Computers Infotech.

(b) Professional Courses (like Engineering & Medical).

(c) Graduation, Post Graduation and other Courses.

Existing recipients of Scholarship will have to apply again for continuance.

Scholarship amount ranges from Rs. 150/- to Rs. 1,000/- per month.

Last Date for Obtaining Application Forms - 20.7.2015

Last Date for Receipt of Completed Forms by the Trust - 30.7.2015

(B)Overseas Loan Scholarship: (For Higher Studies Abroad)

These are stricify for technical subjects to be undertaken by graduates/post graduates in foreign countries. The loan amount is refundable in interest free soft instalments after completion of studies. Loan amount maximum upto Rs. 1,00,000/- lumpsum per selected student.

Eligibility: The students should be of India origin and have a uniformly brilliant academic record.

Last Date for Obtaining Application Forms - 20.5.2015

Last Date for Receipt of Completed Forms by the Trust - 30.5.2015

Application Forms will be available free of charge or can be had by post by sending a 9"x4" self-addressed stamped (Rs. 5/-) envelope to the Secretary, Sahu Jain Trust, 18, Institutional Area, Lodhi Road, New Delhi-110003.
