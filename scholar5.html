N.E.S.T. scholarships: Science, Medical & Engineering students

Exam: 9th Nationwide Education and Scholarship Test (N.E.S.T.) - 2013

Conducted by: SEMCI (India), Mumbai, flag-bearer of the SU Foundation of India

Benefits: Atleast 20 scholarships worth Rs.25,000 to Rs.50,000. Certificates of Appreciation are also awarded to all high scoring candidates in the test with All India Ranking (AIR).

About the test: N.E.S.T comprises of two exams for 2 different categories

NEST-I

Eligibility: Only for First Year and Second Year Degree Engineering, BCA, MBBS, ESc (all) and Final Year Diploma Engineering students Cash Awards: 5 awards of Rs. 15.000 each along with Rs. 25.000 for the highest scorer in this category

NEST-ll :

Eligibility: Only for Third Year and Final Year Degree Engineering, BCA. MBBS, BSO (Comp/ IT) students Cash Awards: 5 awards of Rs. 25,000 each along with Rs. 40,000 for the highest scorer in this category

Note: Certi?cates of Appreciation will be issued to all the candidates, who score above 40% aggregate of all the ?ve sections of the test.

1.NEST I :Physics & Chemistry;

A = Comp/lT/Elex/Telecom/Elect:
