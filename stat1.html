Delhi Technological University (DTU-Delhi) saw the best campus placement in its history when its computer science student, Himanshu Jindal received a placement offer from Google USA with annual pay package of 93 Lakhs (USD 1,15,000) plus 125 stock units from Google USA. 40 companies have visited the campus so far to recruit the students. A total of 265 job offers has been made in the first month itself. The offer made by Google USA is highest till now. Nilesh Agarwal, another final year student DTU Student, has bagged an annual package of USD 1,05,000 (70 Lakh Rupees) from EPIC-A US Based Software Company. Himanshu Jindal expressed his happiness that he got a job offer from a world renowned company like Google. He thanked his parents and friends for the blessings and support. EPIC made job offers to 11 more students including Nilesh Agarwal. Other than EPIC and Google, renowned companies like also made job offers. Goldman Sachs offered a candidate Rs 28 Lakhs and Amazon offered 8 students an annual package of Rs 19 Lakhs. Many leading companies are also offering paid internships to the third year students of the institute.
